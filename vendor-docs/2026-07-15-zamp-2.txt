## Document: Zamp Security Questions - Answers.pdf

Do you have any AI features that may use customer data? If so, what is the policy around that?
● Some features use AI — for example, automated CSV parsing to structure transaction
data, with more planned. AI processing is isolated per customer; one customer's data is
never used to serve another customer.
● AI requests route through Vercel AI Gateway, which operates under a
zero-data-retention policy at the gateway layer when enabled — prompts and outputs
are permanently deleted after each request, not retained by Vercel, and not used to train
Vercel's own models.
● We are evaluating provider-level zero-data-retention enforcement to extend that
guarantee across all downstream model providers.
● A formal AI Usage Policy is in development; in the interim, AI data handling is governed
by our existing data-handling controls.
What 3rd party subprocessors do you use?
● Our primary subprocessors for customer data include AWS (infrastructure/hosting),
PlanetScale (database), Vercel (application hosting and AI Gateway), Browserbase, and
Inngest.
● We are consolidating a complete, maintained subprocessor list in our Trust Center.
When is your SOC 2 certification scheduled to be completed?
● Anticipated Q3
● Our SOC 2 Type II examination is in progress. We can share the report under NDA once
issued. In the meantime, our current control posture is available via our Vanta Trust
Center. https://trust.zamp.com
Do you have any additional documentation regarding data privacy, compliance, security, etc?
● As we finalize documents we will add to our trust center. Our Data Deletion & Erasure
Policy is in progress. Our Vanta Trust Center centralizes our current compliance posture.
